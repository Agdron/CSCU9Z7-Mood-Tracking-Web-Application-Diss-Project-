Welcome to SMART Goal Setting

A SMART goal is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

Swipe or click Next to begin setting your goal.



Specific

Define exactly what you want to do. For example: "take a 15-minute walk." Some suggestions may pop up below.


Suggested Tasks

take a walk practice meditation read for 20 minutes

Measurable

Decide how you will measure progress. For example: "track my steps."


Suggested Tasks

track with an app record in a journal

Achievable

Ensure the goal is realistic and attainable. For example: "set aside time daily."


Suggested Tasks

set a reminder schedule in calendar

Relevant

Explain why this goal matters. For example: "improve my physical and mental well-being."


Suggested Tasks

improve well-being boost mood

Time-bound

Set a deadline. For example: "within 4 weeks."


Suggested Tasks

4 weeks 30 days
